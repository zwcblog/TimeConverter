Privacy Policy for Timestamp Converter Pro

Last Updated: 2024-06-10

1. Introduction

Welcome to Timestamp Converter Pro ("the Extension"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling any personal information we obtain from you with care and respect. This Privacy Policy is designed to answer your questions regarding our privacy practices.

2. Information Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any user data. Timestamp Converter Pro is designed to work entirely locally on your device, without sending any information to external servers.

3. Local Storage

The Extension uses the browser's local storage capabilities to save:

  • User preferences (such as preferred timezone)
  • Conversion history (recent timestamp/date conversions)

This information is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or by using the "Clear History" button within the Extension.

4. Permissions

Timestamp Converter Pro requests the following permissions:

  • activeTab: Used only to enable the copy-to-clipboard functionality.
  • clipboardWrite: Allows the Extension to copy conversion results to your clipboard when you click the "Copy" button.
  • storage: Enables the Extension to store your preferences and conversion history locally on your device.

These permissions are used solely for providing the core functionality of the Extension and not for collecting or transmitting user data.

5. Third-Party Services

Timestamp Converter Pro does not utilize any third-party services, analytics tools, trackers, or advertisements. The Extension operates in isolation and does not communicate with external services.

6. Children's Privacy

Timestamp Converter Pro does not specifically target children under 13, nor do we knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. As we don't collect any user data at all, no personal information of any users, regardless of age, is collected.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. The date of the latest revision will be indicated at the top of the Privacy Policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please reach out to us through the "Support" section on our Chrome Web Store listing or by filing an issue on our GitHub repository.
